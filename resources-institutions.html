Institutions

Institutions

A selection of organizations aimed to inform, guide, and overall support any individual’s needs or issues.

National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI)

National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) strives to limit mental illness by providing a safe and caring environment. NAMI wants to help those currently undergoing any form of mental illness by offering support through their organization. NAMI offers many resources for individuals to find help for themselves and for their community such as articles, hotlines for assistance, news, and more for individuals of all ages.

Addiction Group Impatient Treatment

Addiction Group is dedicated to help individuals suffering from substance abuse and prevent new cases through credible information and spreading awareness to as many as we can reach. They provide a wide range of therapies including Cognitive Behavorial Theraphy, Dialectic Behavior Therapy, Contingency management, and more. Benefits of impatient treatment/rehab also include sense of community, nutritional support, structered programs, and many more.

Mental Health

Mental Health.gov is a website that provides a variety of information concerning health, disorders, anxiety, and others. This site’s goal is to inform individuals about mental health and promote the overall safety of an individual. Mental Health is a site where people of all ages can find help. Additionally, Mental Health.gov allows individuals with ways they can create comfortable situations when confronting mental health.

Make It Ok

A website that sheds light on mental health and helps stimulate positive feelings towards mental illness so that . Make It Ok offers stories and conversation examples when discussing mental health. It allows individuals to confidently discuss mental health and how to offer support for it.

Young Women’s Health

A blog that presents young females with answers to questions about puberty, sexuality, and many other female-related content. Additionally, Young Women’s Health offers ways for women to increase their emotional, physical health and overall wellbeing. Young Women’s Health also offers guides, anecdotes, and personal assistance.

Young Men’s Health

A blog that presents young males with answers to questions about puberty, sexuality, and many other male-related content. Additionally, Young Men’s Health offers ways for women to increase their emotional, physical health and overall well-being. Young Men’s Health also offers guides, anecdotes, and personal assistance.

Go Ask Alice

A website with a large assortment of information from health specialists, health care providers, and other health professionals for adults undergoing mental health. Their site offers health awareness, health education, counselling, and more.

Kelty Mental Health

An institution that helps young people, 12-24, and provides health and wellness resources, services, and support online. They have youth peer support workers who previously have gone through mental challenges, and they will help you through your struggles.

Reach Out

A website with a large array of articles that covers topics including mental health, mental fitness, identity, and bullying. They offer insightful advice and ways to deal with your struggles.

Teen Mental Health

A website that provides a set of resources for teens that increases the understanding of mental health and disorders and decreases the stigma around mental illness.

Campaign Against Living Miserably (CALM)

A movement against suicide and its impact on society. They run a helpline 7 hours a day, 7 days a week, and spread their message.

Crisis Text Line

A text line that offers free 24/7 support for individuals. They also run one of the leading non-profit organizations DoSomething.org with the purpose to aid individuals in their times of need and work to create social and civic changes in the world.

National Eating Disorder Association

The National Eating Disorders Association (NEDA) is one of the largest nonprofit organizations that offers support for those who have an eating disorder. They instigate many campaigns and advocacy to bring awareness to eating disorders and allow individuals to get involved in their own community. NEDA also offers a multitude of information for those seeking recovery and offers a hotline.

Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA)

A website that offers close-range locations and information for individuals seeking treatment facilities in the United States or U.S. Territories for substance addiction and mental health.
